The role of insulating our place from the macro political economy

It was so clearly felt how much love there was in the room for the solar project at Tolaga Bay Area School. Among the financials and motions it is this storytelling that really matters.

I have a little story of our own that, like now being able to learn to swim for most of the year and provide access to power when the next inevitable cyclone rips through, shows the role that can be played to protect us from decisions made outside our local control.

I have more to say about storytelling, but I'll leave that for another event.
